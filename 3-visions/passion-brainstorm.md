# Passion Brainstorm

## Process

Given 5 minutes, and without judging what comes to mind or limiting what's possible, write down things you want to become, do, places to go, things to happen.

With this list you can sort or categorize them however you like. Pick a couple that would make the most impact, and brainstorm what it would take to make them real.

This exercise pairs with `3-visions/` — completed brainstorm items often graduate into visions (long-term direction with no deadlines).

## Cadence

Run quarterly, or whenever the system feels stagnant.
